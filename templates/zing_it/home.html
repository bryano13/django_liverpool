LIVERPOOL FC

Champion of champions:

Founded the 3rd of June of 1892, and 127 years later Liverpool has once more become the best football team in the world after defeating Totteham Hotspur in the Uefa Champions League Final this year. With this win, the team has 6 trophies of this competition in all of its history, placing them third on the top rank of football clubs with more victories on this tournament.

The "Normal" One

But how can we talk about the greatness of this team without ever mentioning the "NORMAL ONE", the man that made it all possible, the strategist, the brain in the bench: Jurgen Klopp!

When Jurgen Klopp first came to the Premier League people were comparing him with the "Special One" Jose Mourinho. His answer to that was, "If he is the special one then I´ll be the Normal One"

Liverpool Theme Song: You´ll Never Walk Alone"

You’ll Never Walk Alone entered the Top 10 ahead of a home game against West Brom on October 19, 1963, and that may have been the first occasion fans sang along with the song. Two weeks later it was No.1, so was played just before kick-off against Leicester. The song spent four weeks at No.1, and nine weeks in the Top 10, but fans continued to sing it long after it dropped out of the charts.
